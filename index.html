About My Passion

Welcome to MakerMind Nexus! I am passionate about 3D printing and believe that everyone should have the opportunity to explore this exciting hobby. That's why I am dedicated to helping those less fortunate by providing them with the machines and materials they need to get started.

I take old 3D printers and rehab them to give them a new life. My goal is to make 3D printing accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation. I document my journey of rehabbing these machines on my YouTube channel, so you can follow along and learn with me.

Follow My Journey on YouTube

Check out my YouTube channel MakerMind Nexus to watch videos of my 3D printing projects, tutorials, and the process of rehabbing old machines.

Stream Schedule

Join me live on my streams every Tuesday and Thursday from 5-7pm CST. During these streams, I work on 3D printing projects, rehab old machines, and answer any questions you might have about 3D printing.

Don't miss out on the fun and learning. Mark your calendars and join the MakerMind Nexus community!
